<center><b>AvaTax AbanteCart Quick Setup Manual</b></center>
<br />
<p>Before you can start using AvaTax in AbanteCart, you need to have account with Avalara and company setup <a href="https://www.avalara.com/integrations/abantecart/" target="_avalara">https://www.avalara.com/integrations/abantecart/</a>
<br/>
Once your account activated please set up in AvaTax Admin Console your <a href="https://help.avalara.com/000_Avalara_AvaTax/Add_Nexus_Jurisdictions" target="_blank">nexus jurisdictions</a> to tell AvaTax when and where to calculate and report taxes for your company. You initially add nexus jurisdictions when you <a href="https://help.avalara.com/000_Avalara_AvaTax/Set_up_Your_Avalara_AvaTax_Account" target="_blank">configure your company tax profile</a>.<br />
<br/></p>
